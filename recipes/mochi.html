Japanese Mochi Icecream

Mochi icecream is a delcious snack that can commonly be found at various grocery stores but heres how you can make it at home!

What you need!

  • 1 1/2 cups vanilla icecream
  • 1 cup sweet glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Few drops of food coloring of your choice (optional)
  • Cornstarch for dusting (potato starch can be used as a substitute)
